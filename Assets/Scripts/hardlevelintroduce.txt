How to Play
In hard mode, the player will have to conquer 5 stages.
During each stage, if the player shoots the fireball outside the golden Cup, they will have to play again from the first level of that stage.

Durante cada etapa, Si el jugador tira la bola de fuego de la Copa de Oro, que tendrá que jugar de nuevo desde el primer nivel de esa etapa.
